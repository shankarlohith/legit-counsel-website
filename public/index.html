    About Us

    Legit Counsel strives to be the right hand of business enthusiasts for all their financial and legal requirements. We assist our clients in their incidental activities such as taxation, business registration, compliances, intellectual property. We aim to be an end to end supportive pillar to our clients so that they can focus on their core activities.

    About Founder

    Legit Counsel was founded by Bhawna Motwani as a legal and financial haven. Bhawna completed her Masters in Commerce from the University of Rajasthan following which she concluded her Company Secretary course. Bhawna worked for over 5 years with both startups and big four industry leaders, before she founded Legit Counsel.
    Bhawna holds a keen interest in understanding client requirements, reasearching and delivering the best possible solution. She is also quite passionate about US taxation and art.
